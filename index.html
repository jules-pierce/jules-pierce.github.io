Welcome to the University of Pennsylvania IEEE Student Branch

We are the student branch of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers at the University of Pennsylvania, a group catering to students interested in electrical engineering and computer science. The focus of UPenn IEEE is to connect students with industry and faculty in the fields of electrical engineering and computer science as well as with other students. Through our events, we seek to promote professional development, provide networking opportunities, and generate interest in engineering.
